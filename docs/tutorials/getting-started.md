# Getting Started with PDFDriveReader

Learn how to open your first PDF and navigate through its pages.

## Prerequisites
- Android device with the PDFDriveReader app installed.
- At least one PDF file stored on your device.

## Your First Reading Session

1. **Launch the App**: Open PDFDriveReader from your app drawer.
2. **Open a PDF from the Library**:
   - The app starts in **Library Mode**.
   - Browse the **Local Storage** tab to see PDFs already on your device.
   - Tap any PDF file name to open it.
3. **Navigate the Document**:
   - The document opens in **Reader Mode** (Immersive View).
   - **Swipe** to scroll through pages.
   - **Pinch** to zoom in on details.
   - **Single Tap** on the screen to reveal the Menu button and other controls.
4. **Change Reading Direction**:
   - Tap the screen to show the UI.
   - Tap the **Menu** button (top-right).
   - Select your preferred direction (e.g., **Right-to-Left** for Manga).
5. **Return to Library**:
   - Tap the screen to show the UI.
   - Tap the **System Back Button** or select **Close Reader** from the menu.

Congratulations! You've successfully opened and customized your first reading session.
