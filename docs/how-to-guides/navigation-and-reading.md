# How-to Navigate and Read PDFs

This guide provides practical recipes for common reading tasks.

## How to Zoom and Reset View
- **Zoom In**: Use two fingers to pinch outward.
- **Zoom Out**: Use two fingers to pinch inward.
- **Reset Zoom**: Double-tap anywhere on the page to return to 100%.

## How to Change Reading Directions
1. Tap the **Menu** button (top-right).
2. Choose from the available directions:
   - **Left-to-Right**: Best for English novels and standard documents.
   - **Right-to-Left**: Best for Arabic, Hebrew, or Japanese Manga.
   - **Top-to-Bottom**: Best for Technical documents and continuous scrolling.

## How to Navigate via Internal Links
- **Jump to Link**: Tap an internal link (e.g., Table of Contents) to jump to its destination.
- **Return to Previous Position**: Swipe back to return to the page you were on before jumping.

## How to Use the Scrollbar
- **Horizontal Scrollbar** (L-to-R or R-to-L): Located at the bottom. Swipe horizontally or drag the handle to jump to a specific page.
- **Vertical Scrollbar** (T-to-B): Located on the right side. Swipe vertically or drag the handle to jump to a specific page.
*Note: The scrollbar disappears 2 seconds after the scrolling gesture ends.*
